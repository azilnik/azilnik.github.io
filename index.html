Ari Zilnik is a player-coach product design leader who ships great software and enjoys niche 90's nostalgia.

Ari is currently Head of Design at Join, the industry-leading decision-tracking software for construction companies.

Before that, Ari built a global design team at Blockdaemon after they acquired his consultancy, Gravy, in 2021. Through Gravy, he led product design for companies like Gradle, Richardson, Veckta, and Formation Bio (formerly TrialSpark).

Ari also worked as a product designer at Jet.com (acquired by Walmart) and Lab49.
